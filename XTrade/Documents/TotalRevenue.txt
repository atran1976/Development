Description:
Total Revenue (Top Line) is the total amount of income a company generates from its core business activities before any expenses are deducted.

Formula:
Total Revenue = Quantity Sold × Price Per Unit
Revenue Growth Rate = ((Current Revenue − Previous Revenue) / Previous Revenue) * 100
​
Key Insights:
- Operating Revenue: Revenue from the primary business activities (e.g., product sales, service fees).
- Non-Operating Revenue: Income from secondary activities like interest income, dividends, or asset sales.
- Other Revenue Streams: Subscription fees, licensing, royalties, or advertising revenue.
