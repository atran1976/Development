Description:
- Market Cap (Market Capitalization) is a metric used to measure the total value of a company's outstanding shares of stock in the market. 
- It represents the company's equity value and is commonly used to determine its size, stability, and growth potential.

Formula: 
- Market Cap = Share Price × Total Outstanding Shares

Key Components:
- Mega Cap (>$200 Billion)
- Large Cap (>$10 Billion)
- Mid Cap ($2 Billion–$10 Billion)
- Small Cap ($300 Million–$2 Billion)
- Micro Cap (<$300 Million)
